Exporting Diagrams

 

 

VisuTuring allows you to export diagrams as JPEG bitmap files.

  1. Open your Turing Machine.
  2. Go to the File menu and select Export Diagram...

  3. Choose a name and location for your diagram.
  4. Press Save.
  5. Your transition diagram will be saved.
